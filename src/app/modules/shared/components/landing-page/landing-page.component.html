Patients often need the three most fundamental features: the ability to book or cancel appointments, the option to get prescription drugs, and simple access to medical consultation information. Shaafi is the only mobile healthcare app which is easily accessible to all its end-users by offering a hotline number and customer service for issue resolution. Moreover, you can use an agent's assistance to make reservations. Shaafi also deliver medications to your door anywhere in the country. Always at your service, you may also order diagnostic tests on Shaafi and take advantage of free home sample collection. Having access to Shaafi opens the door for an opportunity to get medical advice from the top experts nationwide that too at the comfort of your home or office without waiting in long queues for obtaining the appointments. After consultation you may obtain the e-prescription and order medicines or labtests if needed on the same app.
